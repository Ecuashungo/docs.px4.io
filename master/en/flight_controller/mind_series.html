# (Air)Mind Series

AirMind® have created two new controller series branched from the Pixhawk Series.

  • MindPX - revised schematic and structure, and been further enhanced with new features to make un-manned vehicle more smart and friendly to use.
  • MindRacer - a fully stackable flight platform for miniature UAVs.
    • This is a platform, which currently has two RTF vehicles: MindRacer 210 and NanoMind 110
