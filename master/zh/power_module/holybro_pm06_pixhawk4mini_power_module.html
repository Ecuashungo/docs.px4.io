# Holybro Micro Power Module (PM06)

This power module provides regulated power for a flight controller and ESCs, and sends information to the autopilot about the battery’s voltage and current draw. It is supplied with the Pixhawk 4 Mini, but can also be separately purchased.

# 产品规格

  • PCB Current: 120A continued
  • UBEC Current: 3A Max
  • Power input: DC 7V~42V(2S~10S)
  • Power output: DC 5.1V~5.3V
  • Dimensions: 35x35x5mm
  • Mounting hole: 30.5mm*30.5mm
  • Weight: 7g

# Package Contents

  • PM06 board (1)
  • 80mm XT60 connector wire (1)
  • 6pin GH cable (1)
  • 6pin JST cable (1)

# 采购

PM06 V2 Power Management Board (opens new window)

# Wiring/Connections

Wiring and connection examples can be found in: Pixhawk 4 Mini > Power.
