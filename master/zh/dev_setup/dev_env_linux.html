# Linux环境下的开发

Linux允许您构建所有PX4目标(基于NuttX的硬件、高通骁龙飞行硬件、基于Linux的硬件、仿真、ROS)。

提示

Ubuntu Linux LTS LTS 18.04 and 20.04 are supported. Other Linux environments and versions may be made to work, but are less well tested and documented.
